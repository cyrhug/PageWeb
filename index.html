cyril huguenin is a pianist exploring the boundaries between contemporary classical, jazz, punk, and who is looking to experiment and interrogate the means of performing and listening, trying to change the conventions of the traditional western concert ritual.

they co-founded 'ensemble vasistas' and 'jean-sébastien', with which they are active in brussels and belgium.
